What is the Menstrual Cycle?

The menstrual cycle is a monthly hormonal cycle that prepares the body for pregnancy. The average cycle length is 28 days but can range from 21â€“35 days.

Four Phases

  • Menstrual Phase: Shedding of uterine lining
  • Follicular Phase: Egg develops
  • Ovulation: Egg released
  • Luteal Phase: Body prepares for next cycle

Self Care Tips

  • Stay hydrated
  • Light exercise helps cramps
  • Balanced diet improves energy
  • Track mood changes

Myths vs Facts

Myth: You shouldn't exercise during period.
Fact: Light exercise can reduce cramps.
