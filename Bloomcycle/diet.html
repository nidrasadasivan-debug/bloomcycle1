Hormones peak. Eat anti-inflammatory foods.

  • Avocados
  • Salmon & omega-3 foods

  • Seeds (chia, flax)
Focus: Antioxidants & healthy fats

Luteal Phase (Before Period)

Energy drops. PMS may occur.

  • Complex carbs (sweet potatoes)
  • Magnesium-rich foods (nuts, bananas)
  • High-fiber foods
  • Plenty of water
Avoid: Sugary snacks & processed foods
